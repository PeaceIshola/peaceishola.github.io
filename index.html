Research Interest

I specialize in dealing with magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) data from the brain, which is a medical imaging method used in radiology. To acquire comprehensive pictures of the brain, MRI scanners use intense magnetic fields, magnetic field gradients, and radio waves. My particular interest is in using machine learning models and algorithms to evaluate brain MRI data and identify the areas that contribute to fluid intelligence. My objective is to combine functional connectivity networks, which will allow us to view and comprehend the relationships of various brain areas. I want to contribute to the disciplines of precision medicine, fluid intelligence research, brain region interaction investigations, and customized healthcare via this effort. This study has the potential to advance our knowledge of the brain and improve healthcare outcomes, which excites me.
